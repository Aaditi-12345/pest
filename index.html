Ant Control

Eliminate colonies at the source

The Problem

Ants can quickly infest your home, contaminating food and creating unsightly trails. Colonies can have thousands of workers with hidden nests.

Our Treatment Process
  1. Thorough inspection to locate nests and trails
  2. Application of eco-friendly gel baits
  3. Barrier treatment around entry points
  4. Follow-up inspection if needed
Safety Measures
  • Pet-safe gel formulations
  • Non-toxic to children once dry
  • No harsh chemical odors
  • Environmentally responsible
Results & Duration

Visible reduction within 24-48 hours. Complete colony elimination in 5-7 days. Treatment includes warranty coverage.

Cockroach Control

Fast elimination of all species

The Problem

Cockroaches spread bacteria, trigger allergies, and reproduce rapidly. German and American roaches are common in Gold Coast homes.

Our Treatment Process
  1. Identify species and harborage areas
  2. Apply odorless residual spray to surfaces
  3. Place gel baits in cracks and crevices
  4. Treat outdoor areas to prevent re-entry
Safety Measures
  • Kitchen-safe formulations
  • Odorless and stain-free
  • Safe for pets after drying
  • Low toxicity products
Results & Duration

Significant reduction within 24 hours. Complete elimination in 1-2 weeks. Protection includes warranty coverage.

Spider Control

Web removal & residual protection

The Problem

Spiders including Huntsmans and Redbacks can invade homes. While most are harmless, they create webs and can be frightening for families.

Our Treatment Process
  1. Remove existing webs from all areas
  2. Apply residual spray to interior surfaces
  3. Treat exterior perimeter and entry points
  4. Focus on roof voids and subfloor areas
Safety Measures
  • Low-odor formulations
  • Safe for children and pets
  • No staining on surfaces
  • Environmentally conscious
Results & Duration

Immediate web removal. Residual protection kills spiders on contact. Full warranty included.

Rodent Control

Humane trapping & exclusion

The Problem

Rats and mice cause property damage, contaminate food, and spread diseases. They can squeeze through tiny gaps and multiply quickly.

Our Treatment Process
  1. Inspect for entry points and activity signs
  2. Set humane traps in strategic locations
  3. Seal entry points to prevent re-entry
  4. Remove and dispose of captured rodents
Safety Measures
  • Humane trapping methods
  • Child-proof bait stations
  • Pet-safe placement
  • No toxic sprays indoors
Results & Duration

Traps checked within 48 hours. Most infestations resolved in 1-2 weeks. Proofing provides long-term protection with warranty.

Flea Control

Complete life cycle elimination

The Problem

Fleas cause itchy bites for humans and pets. A single female can lay hundreds of eggs, creating rapid infestations in carpets and pet bedding.

Our Treatment Process
  1. Treat all carpets and soft furnishings
  2. Apply growth regulator to break life cycle
  3. Treat outdoor areas where pets rest
  4. Follow-up treatment if necessary
Safety Measures
  • Safe for pets after drying
  • Non-staining formulas
  • Low odor products
  • Family-friendly treatment
Results & Duration

Adult fleas killed within hours. Eggs and larvae eliminated over 2-3 weeks. Complete control typically achieved in one treatment with warranty.

Wasp & Bee Removal

Safe nest elimination

The Problem

Wasps and bees can build nests in roofs, walls, and trees. Stings can be dangerous, especially for those with allergies. Nests grow rapidly in summer.

Our Treatment Process
  1. Assess nest location and accessibility
  2. Apply targeted treatment at dusk/dawn
  3. Remove nest after insects are eliminated
  4. Treat area to prevent rebuilding
Safety Measures
  • Professional protective equipment
  • Safe distance treatment
  • Family kept indoors during treatment
  • Complete nest removal
Results & Duration

Nest activity stops within 24 hours. Complete removal same day or next visit. Guaranteed nest elimination with warranty.
